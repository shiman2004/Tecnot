About Tecnot

Tecnot is a university SDGP project developed to improve the workflow of healthcare professionals by automating clinical note-taking. The app converts voice into structured SOAP notes in real-time, ensuring speed, accuracy, and convenience.

Developed by a team of passionate computer science students at the University of Moratuwa, Tecnot aims to solve the documentation challenges faced by Sri Lankan clinicians through innovative AI technology.
