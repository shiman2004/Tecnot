About Tecnot

Built for doctors — faster notes, cleaner workflows, more time for care.

Doctor-first documentation

Tecnot is an AI-powered clinical documentation assistant designed for doctors and healthcare professionals.

It listens to consultations and converts them into structured SOAP notes in real time — reducing documentation time while maintaining clinical accuracy.

By minimizing manual note-taking, Tecnot helps clinicians focus more on patient care and less on administrative workload.
